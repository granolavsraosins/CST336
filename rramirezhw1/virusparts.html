Parts of a Virus

Home Computer Virus Phases Five Known Virus Types Parts of a Virus


Computer viruses have 3 main parts to them and each contain a search and copy routine which tell the virus to infect certain computers and copy itself.

An infection mechanism is how a virus spreads by using its search routine that looks for new victims.

A trigger can be a malicious program disguised as a legitimate one. It con contain specific instructions before it activates.

The payload would be data that actively does harm to a computer and slows down usage.
